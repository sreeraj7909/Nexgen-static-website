Services

we provide the bests

WTP (WATER TREATMENT PLANT)

Water purification removes impurities to make water suitable for specific purposes, guided by standards for contaminant levels based on its intended use.

STP (Sewage Treatment Plant)

Sewage treatment removes contaminants from municipal wastewater through physical, chemical, and biological processes, producing safe treated wastewater.

ETP (Effluent Treatment Plant)

Our turnkey Effluent Treatment Plants (ETP) efficiently handle diverse wastewater types with compact, portable designs, utilizing advanced physical-chemical treatments for contaminants.

RO (REVERSE OSMOSIS PLANT)

Reverse osmosis (RO) purifies water by using a semi-permeable membrane to remove ions, molecules, and particles, retaining solutes on one side while allowing pure solvent to pass through.

NEXGEN WATER PURIFIERS (RO+UV, UV+UF, RO+ALKALINE)

Our RO+UV+UF+TDS Control+ UV disinfection process guarantees 100% pure drinking water by removing dissolved impurities like arsenic, rust, pesticides, and fluorides, while killing bacteria and viruses. Double purification with UV and UF ensures absolute safety. The water revitalizer improves hydration and mineral absorption.

Water Testing and Analysis

Conducting comprehensive tests to assess the quality of water, identifying contaminants, and determining the appropriate treatment methods.

Maintenance and Repair Services

Providing ongoing maintenance, troubleshooting, and repair services for water treatment equipment and systems to ensure optimal performance and longevity.

Consulting and Compliance

Offering consulting services to help clients navigate regulatory requirements, obtain permits, and ensure compliance with water quality standards and regulations.

Water Softening

Providing solutions to mitigate hard water problems by removing excess minerals such as calcium and magnesium ions.
